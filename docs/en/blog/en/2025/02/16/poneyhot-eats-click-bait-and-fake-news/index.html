PoneyHot - eats click bait and fake news

February 16, 2025

The internet is full of fake news and clickbait, making it hard to find trustworthy information. PoneyHot helps you cut through the noise by summarizing content and fact-checking claims—quickly and easily.

What is PoneyHot?

PoneyHot is a tool designed to help you get straight to the facts. Whether you’re browsing online articles or checking viral claims, PoneyHot makes it effortless to separate fact from fiction.

It works in two simple ways:

  • Summarization: Quickly condenses articles, so you don’t waste time on unnecessary fluff.
  • Fact-Checking: Instantly verifies claims, helping you spot misinformation.

How to Use PoneyHot

Firefox Extension

  • Summarize Articles: Right-click a link and choose “Summarize”—PoneyHot will generate a short, clear summary.
  • Fact-Check Claims: Highlight a statement, right-click, and select “Fact Check” to verify it instantly.

Telegram Bot

  • Send a URL to the bot, and it will return a summary.
  • Prefix a message with ??? for a fact check or !!! for a summary.
  • Share links directly from your mobile device for instant analysis.

Why Use PoneyHot?

  • Save Time: Skip long articles and get straight to the key points.
  • Avoid Clickbait: Know what an article is really about before you click.
  • Verify Information: Don’t fall for fake news—fact-check with ease.

Take Control of Your Online Experience

PoneyHot helps you stay informed without the noise. Whether you’re reading news, researching topics, or verifying claims, PoneyHot ensures you get clear, reliable, and fact-checked information—fast.

Try PoneyHot today and browse with confidence!
